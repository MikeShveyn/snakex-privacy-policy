1. Introduction

SnakeX is a mobile game developed for entertainment purposes. This game does not collect or store personal data from users. SnakeX is fully offline, but uses Unity Ads to offer rewarded ad content (e.g., continue after game over, bonus coins).

2. Data Collected

SnakeX itself does not collect any personal information such as name, email, location, or contacts.

However, Unity Ads SDK integrated in the game may collect limited anonymous data to serve relevant ads and track performance.

  • Advertising ID (Android)
  • Device type and OS version
  • Gameplay session duration
  • Country (non-precise location)

Unity’s data collection is governed by their Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use the Data

We do not process or store any data ourselves. Ad-related anonymous data is used by Unity for:

  • Displaying non-personalized or personalized ads (based on user choice)
  • Measuring ad performance

4. Children’s Privacy

SnakeX is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we’ll remove it.

5. Your Consent

By using SnakeX, you consent to this privacy policy and to Unity Ads' data practices as described. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.
